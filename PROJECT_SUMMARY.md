## 🌟 Key Innovation: CWT-LSTM Autoencoder

### Why This Approach Works
1. **🌊 Continuous Wavelet Transform**: Captures time-frequency evolution of gravitational wave chirps
2. **🤖 LSTM Autoencoder**: Learns normal noise patterns, detects anomalous GW signals  
3. **📊 Unsupervised Learning**: No need for labeled training data
4. **🎯 Precision Optimized**: Low false alarm rate suitable for astronomy

### Technical Breakthrough
- **Novel application** of CWT + deep learning to gravitational wave detection
- **Unsupervised anomaly detection** in time-frequency domain
- **Template-free detection** that can discover unknown signal types
- **Scalable approach** that improves with signal strength
